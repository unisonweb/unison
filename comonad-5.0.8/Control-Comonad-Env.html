Description

The Env comonad (aka the Coreader, Environment, or Product comonad)

A co-Kleisli arrow in the Env comonad is isomorphic to a Kleisli arrow in the reader monad.

(a -> e -> m) ~ (a, e) -> m ~ Env e a -> m
