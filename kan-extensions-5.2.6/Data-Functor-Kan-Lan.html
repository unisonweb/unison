The universal property of a left Kan extension.

fromLan :: (forall a. Lan g h a -> f a) -> h b -> f (g b) Source #

fromLan and toLan witness a (higher kinded) adjunction between Lan g and (Compose g)

toLan . fromLan ≡ id
fromLan . toLan ≡ id
